My name is Kate, and I am a first year Ph.D. student at Johns Hopkins University. I am co-advised by [Professor Benjamin Van Durme](https://www.cs.jhu.edu/~vandurme/) and [Professor Alan Yuille](http://www.cs.jhu.edu/~ayuille/) of the [JHU Center for Language and Speech Processing](https://www.clsp.jhu.edu/). I am interested in natural language processing, computer vision, and cognitive science. My current research is focused on multimodal AI and uncertainty in machine learning.


I received a BA in Computer Science from UC Berkeley, where I conducted AI and robotics research at the [UC Berkeley AUTOLab](http://autolab.berkeley.edu/) and was advised by [Professor Ken Goldberg](https://goldberg.berkeley.edu/).
